Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 22, 2026

Our Privacy Promise

FuelCast is owned, operated, and maintained by Gregory Yuzik ("us" or "we"). Your privacy matters to us. This policy explains what data FuelCast collects, how it's used, and your rights.

What We Collect

Data Type Collected? Details
Location On-device only Used to find gas stations near you. Your location is sent to Apple Maps to search for stations. It is never sent to our servers.
Fuel Prices From EIA.gov Regional average prices are fetched from the U.S. Energy Information Administration's public API. No personal data is sent with these requests.
Favorites On-device only Your favorite stations are stored locally on your device using UserDefaults. We never receive this data.
Names or Emails No We do not collect any personally identifiable information.
Usage Analytics No We do not track how you use the app.

Advertising

FuelCast displays ads served by Google AdMob to support the app as a free service. Here's what you should know:

  • Contextual ads only. FuelCast does not request App Tracking Transparency (ATT) permission. We do not track you across apps or websites. Ads are served based on the app category (automotive/fuel), not your personal browsing history.
  • What Google may collect: Google AdMob may collect device information (device model, OS version), IP address (for approximate location-based ad targeting), and ad interaction data (impressions, taps). This data is governed by Google's Privacy Policy.
  • No personal profiles. Since we do not enable tracking, Google cannot build a cross-app advertising profile from FuelCast.
  • Ad-free option. You can purchase the ad-free upgrade within FuelCast. Once purchased, the Google AdMob SDK is completely disabled and Google no longer collects any data from the app on your device.

No Tracking. No Analytics. No Exceptions.

Beyond the Google AdMob SDK (which serves contextual ads only), FuelCast contains zero analytics frameworks and zero tracking technologies. We do not build profiles, and we do not sell data — because we don't have any to sell.

Device Permissions

FuelCast requests the following permission:

  • Location (When In Use) — Used to find gas stations near you via Apple Maps. Your location is sent only to Apple's servers for the search. It is never sent to us. If you deny location access, the app uses a default location (San Francisco).

Third-Party Services

  • Apple MapKit — Searches for gas stations near your location. Requests are processed by Apple's servers.
  • U.S. Energy Information Administration (EIA) — Provides weekly regional fuel price data via a public API. No personal data is included in these requests.
  • Google AdMob — Serves contextual advertisements. Disabled entirely if you purchase the ad-free upgrade. See Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Apple StoreKit — Handles the ad-free in-app purchase. All payment information is managed by Apple. We never receive your payment details.

Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children under 13. FuelCast does not require an account and collects no personally identifiable information.

Your Rights

Regardless of where you live, FuelCast's privacy-first design means your rights are protected:

  • Right of Access — Your favorites and preferences are stored locally on your device. You have full access at all times.
  • Right to Erasure — Delete the app to remove all local data.
  • Right to Object — Purchase the ad-free upgrade to completely disable Google AdMob data collection.

For users in the European Economic Area (EEA), these rights are guaranteed under GDPR. For users in California, CCPA applies. Since FuelCast collects no personal data directly, no data processing agreement is required.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by updating the "Last Updated" date above. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at support@fuelfinder.app.
