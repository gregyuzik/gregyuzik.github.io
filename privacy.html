Privacy Policy

Data Handling Overview

Klosyt utilizes on-device processing and Apple's secure iCloud infrastructure to protect user privacy. All images and wardrobe data reside solely within your private iCloud account. At no point is your data collected by, transmitted to, or shared with Klosyt or external third parties.

Location & Weather Data

To act as your personal AI stylist, Klosyt requests location data solely to fetch accurate local weather conditions via Apple WeatherKit. This ensures your outfit suggestions are appropriate for the day's forecast. We never track, store, or transmit your precise location to our servers. All weather queries are handled securely through Apple's native services, and your location is processed entirely on-device.
